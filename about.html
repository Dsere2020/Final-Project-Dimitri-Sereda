About Trust Wallet

We make it easy for anyone to buy, sell and understand cryptocurrencies.

In short, we make getting into crypto easy! We started out as a virtual Bitcoin ATM solution provider in 2014 but quickly pivoted to become a cryptocurrency trading platform. At Trust Wallet, you can now trade 20+ cryptocurrencies 24/7 through our Web and Mobile apps.

We are also world's first publicly owned crypto trading platform that is fully regulated. Getting regulated was important to us because we believe that the future of crypto is safe, compliant and secure.

WHAT WE'RE ABOUT

Briliant Basics

Whether you're a beginner or have some trading chops, we're here to make things easier, not harder for you. We believe in keeping things intentionally simple and making crypto welcoming. With that in mind, we've designed our crypto trading platform to be really straightforward, so that you can start your crypto journey quickly and easily, regardless of your knowledge level.

Education is important too! To help, we've put together a Crypto Academy that helps outline many of the crypto basics you should know about when getting started.

Being a Trusted Partner

We want to empower you with resources that help make your crypto journey a breeze. Part of that is building a trusted, fully regulated environment for you to get started in. We prioritize safety over speedy shortcuts, and won't hide behind technical jargon that confuses you.

We've also built in some feedback loops to make sure that we have a pulse on what our users need or are saying. If you ever have questions or need support, our warm and helpful customer support team are available to help you seven days a week. Just reach out and they'll get back to you as soon as possible.
